# Agile Delivery Timeline & Sprint Overview

## Project Timeline

### Phase 1: Discovery & Planning (Weeks 1-3)
- **Week 1:** Requirements gathering and stakeholder interviews
- **Week 2:** Business analysis and market research
- **Week 3:** Agile user stories creation and backlog refinement

### Phase 2: Development (Weeks 4-8)
- **Sprint 1 (Week 4):** Password reset feature development
- **Sprint 2 (Week 5):** Order tracking UI implementation
- **Sprint 3 (Week 6):** Backend order tracking API
- **Sprint 4 (Week 7):** Real-time notifications setup
- **Sprint 5 (Week 8):** Integration and cross-feature testing

### Phase 3: Launch & Optimization (Weeks 9+)
- **Week 9:** Staging environment testing and fixes
- **Week 10:** Production deployment
- **Week 11+:** Monitoring, user feedback, optimization

## Backlog Grooming Sessions
- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly (every 2 weeks)
- **Duration:** 1.5 hours
- **Attendees:** Product, Engineering, QA leads
- **Outcomes:** Refined user stories, clear acceptance criteria, story points assigned

## Sprint Planning Approach
1. **Sprint Planning Meeting:** 2 hours (start of each sprint)
2. **Daily Standups:** 15 minutes (Monday-Friday)
3. **Sprint Review:** 1 hour (end of sprint)
4. **Sprint Retrospective:** 1 hour (end of sprint)

## Key Metrics Tracked
- **Burndown Chart:** Story points completed vs. sprint goal
- **Velocity:** Average story points per sprint
- **Cycle Time:** Time from story creation to completion
- **Defect Rate:** Bugs found post-deployment
- **Customer Satisfaction:** NPS and user feedback

## Stakeholder Communication
- **Weekly Status Updates:** Email summary to leadership
- **Bi-weekly Demo:** Product and Marketing team showcase
- **Monthly Town Halls:** Full team updates and celebrations
